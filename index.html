About Us

Supreme Info Consult – Ghana (SICG) is committed to enhancing the skills and impact of information professionals, including librarians, archivists, systems analysts, database administrators, and IT experts. We also provide basic IT training programs for tertiary and senior high school students.

Vision

To be a leading hub for empowering information professionals and students in Ghana and beyond

Mission

To deliver practical consulting and training that builds skills, confidence, and innovation in libraries, archives, IT, and education
